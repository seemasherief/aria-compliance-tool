EU AI Act — Technical Documentation
Auto-generated · CS Chatbot v2.4 · Requires human review before submission

1. System Overview
CS Chatbot v2.4 is a conversational AI deployed in e-commerce customer service. It processes inbound enquiries, automates common requests, and routes complex cases to human agents 24/7 across web, mobile, and messaging channels.

2. Risk Classification
Under EU AI Act Annex III: classified as Limited Risk under Article 6(2). Transparency obligations under Article 52 apply mandatorily. Reclassification review recommended due to automated financial decisions.

3. Transparency Gap (Art. 52)
⚠ GAP IDENTIFIED: Current deployment does not include mandatory AI disclosure. Recommended fix: insert disclosure within first response. Approved text: "You are being assisted by an AI system. A human agent is available on request."

4. Human Oversight (Art. 14)
Human escalation pathway exists via /agent command. Written procedure specifying conditions for mandatory human review is pending — this is a critical gap requiring immediate remediation.
