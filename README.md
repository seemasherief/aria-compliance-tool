# ARIA - AI Risk & Compliance Intelligence
### Built by Seema Sherief

A compliance risk mitigation tool for e-commerce Customer Service AI systems.

🔗 **[View Live Tool](https://seemasherief.github.io/aria-compliance-tool)**

---

## What This Tool Does

ARIA helps AI Compliance Managers proactively monitor, assess and remediate compliance risks in CS AI systems - covering EU AI Act, GDPR and CCPA requirements.

---

## The 6 Modules

| Module | Purpose |
|---|---|
| 01 Dashboard | Live risk score, alerts and system health overview |
| 02 AI Assessment | Structured compliance audits with EU AI Act checklist |
| 03 Interaction Analyzer | Real-time compliance flagging of CS conversations |
| 04 Regulatory Map | EU AI Act · GDPR · CCPA cross-reference matrix |
| 05 Doc Generator | Auto-generate DPIAs, Technical Docs, Oversight Manuals |
| 06 Remediation Tracker | Prioritised action plans with ownership and timelines |

---

## Regulations Covered

- **EU AI Act** - Art. 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 43, 52
- **GDPR** - Art. 5, 6, 22, 30, 32, 33, 35
- **CCPA / CPRA** - §1798.100, §1798.105, §1798.120, §1798.150

## About This Project

This is a portfolio prototype demonstrating AI governance workflow design, regulatory knowledge and product thinking for AI Compliance roles.

Data is simulated to represent realistic e-commerce CS AI compliance scenarios. In production this would connect to live CS platforms via API with an LLM handling real-time compliance analysis.
